Who we are

The Story

Angel Skars came up the way real bands do — in basements and back rooms, on borrowed amps, under a banner painted by hand.

Two long-haired degenerates in fur and leather, chasing the sound of a city that never sleeps and never shuts up. Glam on the outside, sleaze underneath.

From Arlene's Grocery to the Cobra Club, they've been grinding the downtown rock circuit one sweaty room at a time — building something loud, ugly, and impossible to ignore.
